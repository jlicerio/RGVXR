HIRO Marker for AR.js

Instructions:

  • Display this page on another device (laptop, tablet, etc.)
  • Make sure the marker is well-lit and not reflective
  • Hold your camera steady and point it at the marker
  • Keep the marker square in view (don't view from extreme angles)
  • Maintain a reasonable distance (not too close, not too far)
  • Avoid covering any part of the marker
